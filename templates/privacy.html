Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information you provide directly when using our service, including:

• Account information: name, email address, and password credentials

• Financial data: expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions

• Usage data: how you interact with the application and features

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve our expense tracking service. Specifically:

• To store and organize your expense records

• To generate spending insights and reports

• To authenticate your account and secure your data

• To communicate with you about service updates

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

You retain full ownership of all data you input into Spendly. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

• With service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., hosting services)

• When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

• With your explicit consent

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the application.
